How it works
Five steps.
One closed loop.

From the second your meeting starts to the moment every task is confirmed done — MeetingMap runs the whole chain automatically.

01
Capture
AI bot joins Zoom, Meet, or Teams silently. Live transcription begins. Zero setup for attendees.
›
02
Confirm
Host gets action cards instantly after the call. Edit, merge, or approve — nothing moves without your sign-off.
›
03
Distribute
Tasks auto-created in Jira, Asana, or Linear. Every assignee gets a context-rich email in seconds.
›
04
Sync
Complete in Jira or in MeetingMap — both stay in sync via webhooks. One source of truth, always.
›
05
Track & Close
Host sets check-in frequency. Nudges fire automatically. Loop closes when every task is done.
